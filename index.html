Introduction
Press "w", "s", "a", "d" to accelerate, decelerate, turn left, and turn right.
The speed and acceleration of the car are displayed at the bottom.
Blue parts represent the starting line of a lap
Red parts represent curbs
Green parts represent grass with lower friction.
Black parts represent high friction zones.
White parts represent race track.
You could switch between different tracks with "Change Track" button
Tracks could be customized with any image drawn in red, green, blue, black and white.
Put your image inside the folder and append your config in track.js.
